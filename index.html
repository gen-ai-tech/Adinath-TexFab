Our Story

Rooted in Erode, Woven with Pride

For over two decades, Adinath Texofab has been the heart of authentic textiles in Erode. We collaborate directly with master weavers and artisans, preserving centuries-old techniques while embracing contemporary elegance.

Every thread tells a story of dedication, from handpicked silks to ethically sourced cottons. Our showroom is a destination for connoisseurs who value heritage and fine craftsmanship.
